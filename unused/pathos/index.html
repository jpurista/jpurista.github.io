The Pathos AI

Pathos is an AI that can talk to you whenever you need to vent or a shoulder to cry on.


Pathos is trained with crisis hotline scripts as well as other scripts & phrases that are widely used by Psychiatrists, Psychologists, and counselors.



The Pathos Machine

The machine takes full advantage of the AI to create a safe, calm, and trusting environment.






Implementation

The AI

Two modes: crisis & scheduled

Crisis Mode is meant to serve as an alternative to support individuals who would benefit from using the National Suicide Prevention Online Chat. The user would be able to have a mostly normal or natural conversation with the AI.

Scheduled Mode is meant for users to have someone to regularly talk to. The AI would be there to mostly listen to one's story and feelings while providing some response (that does not act as a recommendation of treatment).

The Machine

The physical embodiment of the AI

The machine is meant to be placed in a semi-private communal setting such as study room in a university or quiet pod in the workplace.

The machine will have all of the features of the AI while also providing a safe, quiet space for an individual to recieve support, whether regularyl or on a one-time basis.
